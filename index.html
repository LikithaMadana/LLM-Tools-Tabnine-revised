About Foodistan

Foodistan is a family-owned restaurant that has been serving delicious Indian cuisine since 1989. Our chefs use only the freshest ingredients to create authentic dishes that will take your taste buds on a journey through India. We pride ourselves on our friendly service and welcoming atmosphere, making us the perfect place to enjoy a meal with friends or family.
